Free as in Freedom

Today I am relieved; both from my company and from dated development jobs. After I witnessed a massive fuck-up of a major project by the management team, I finally moved out of the company, and other similar companies. I would never want to work in those companies where poor management decisions overrule any good technical work, where engineers are horded instead of valued.

I am going to work for very small company where passion for tech drives the employees. Where each member would have the absolute freedom to work from wherever they please as long as they are productive. Where there would be no bar on sites which you can visit. Where you just don't work, but learn everyday as you work;
